My name is Bruno. I'm a senior product designer based in Brazil with 6+ years of experience leading innovative projects across multiple industries.

I didn’t take the typical path into product design — I started with a Mathematics degree, then sharpened my technical skills as a front-end developer. Working closely with engineers gave me a deep respect for how products are built, but it was discovering UX and UI that truly clicked for me. I realized that great products don’t just function well — they connect with people. Since then, I’ve combined my analytical mindset, coding background, and user-first approach to design solutions that are intuitive, purposeful, and grounded in real human needs. Curiosity drives me, collaboration inspires me, and research is always at the core of what I build. I love being hands-on, turning ideas into real, usable products that make a difference.

Skilled in UX/UI design, generative AI, and front-end development, I focus on crafting user-centric, high-impact digital experiences.
