What this is

A practitioner teaching practitioners.

This isn't an abstract AI hype talk. These sessions are built from real experience building AI-powered products at Dell Technologies and running hands-on workshops for professionals across disciplines. Every module is crafted for teams who need to understand and work with AI — not just hear about it. Whether you're in design, product, engineering, marketing, or operations, the goal is the same: practical fluency you can use on Monday morning.

Workshop modules

What your team will learn

Sessions can be delivered individually or as a full series. Each module is practical, illustrated, and tailored to your team's context.

Module 1
🧠

How AI Really Works

A clear, jargon-free foundation: from AI and ML concepts to deep learning, generative AI, and how large language models actually process language.

  • AI / ML / DL / GenAI distinctions
  • How LLMs work: tokenization & embeddings
  • Decoding strategies & temperature
  • Hallucination: types, benchmarks, mitigation
Module 2
⚡

Prompt Engineering

Move from guessing to systematic results. Learn how to craft prompts that consistently produce the output you need.

  • Single-use, single-task & complex prompts
  • POWERD methodology (Persona, Output, Who, Examples, Refinement, Directives)
  • Conversational & iterative method
  • Hands-on practice session
Module 3
🔗

AI Architecture & Agents

Go deeper into how AI systems are built. Understand the building blocks behind products your team ships or uses every day.

  • RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
  • AI Agents & tool use
  • Memory systems & data pipelines
  • Fine-tuning vs. prompting trade-offs
Module 4
🛠️

AI in Your Craft

Practical AI applied to how you work. This module focuses on the tools most relevant to your team's discipline — adapting AI into real workflows, not theoretical ones.

  • AI-native tools for your area (design, code, content, ops, and more)
  • Hands-on practice with the tool stack your team actually uses
  • Designing workflows that keep humans in the loop
  • Evaluating AI output quality in your specific context
