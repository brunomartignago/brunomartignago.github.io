Maplestory Progression App

This was a fun challenge I made for myself. Maplestory is my favorite game of all time and one of the main problems with the game is the amount of systems and components you need to learn in order to get better. This app is an idea on how the company behind the game could help the player base keep track of their progress in a fun and easy way.
